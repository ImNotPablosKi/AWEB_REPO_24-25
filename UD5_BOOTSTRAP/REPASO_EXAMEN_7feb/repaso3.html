Sister Friede is a Boss enemy in Dark Souls 3. She is part of the Ashes of Ariandel DLC. This Dark Souls 3 Sister Friede Guide features locations, strategies and videos on how to defeat Sister Friede easily, as well as tips, weaknesses, trivia and lore notes for the Sister Friede boss. Sister Friede is dressed in nun's clothing and wields a scythe when fighting the player. She fights alone in the first and third phases of the boss fight and alongside Father Ariandel, a hulking figure who attacks with a large bowl, in the second phase. Rather than having two separate health bars, Friede and Ariandel share a single health bar when fighting together, making the second phase unique in its mechanics
